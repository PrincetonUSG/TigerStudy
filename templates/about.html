How do I find my classes?

We pull course data from Princeton OIT's Web Feeds, so you'll be able to find all courses on the Home page search panel.

How do I join a group?

When you sign up for a course, you'll be placed with a study group. An email will be automatically sent to your Princeton email address. If you don't receive an email shortly after joining a group, please reach out to tiger-study@princeton.edu.

How do I view my groups?

View your groups in the My Groups page. Here you can click on a group to view your group members, switch to a different group, or leave the group.

By using this service, you are agreeing to respect all of Princeton's collaboration policies, including general Honor Code rules and also course-specific guidelines.
