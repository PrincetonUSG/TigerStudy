Would you benefit from meeting with a peer to help keep you accountable as you pursue your goals and aspirations? Do you want to offer supportive accountability to a fellow student? Check out the McGraw Center's Study Partners program to learn more!

Step 1


Start by searching above for the classes you are taking. Find your classes!

Step 2


Click on the "Join" button to join a group. You'll be instantly placed into a group and we will send you an email with your partnering details.

Step 3


Find a time that suits your group, and start studying together. Be sure to follow course policies on collaboration!
